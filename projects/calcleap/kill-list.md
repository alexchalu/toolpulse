# CalcLeap — Kill List

Ideas, strategies, and tactics that are PERMANENTLY off-limits. Each entry has a date, the reason, and the evidence.

**Rule:** An entry is added only by the `weekly-retro` skill. Once added, it cannot be re-attempted without an explicit retro entry stating what changed since the last refutation.

## Killed strategies

### KS-1: Mass calculator page production for SEO traffic (added 2026-04-19)
- **Refutation evidence:** 3,012 pages produced; 5 indexed; 0 traffic; AdSense rejected for "low value content."
- **Why it cannot be retried:** The bottleneck is upstream (Google trust signals), not page count. Producing more pages amplifies the trust problem rather than solving it.
- **What would have to change to retry:** A new domain with established authority, OR a fundamentally different content format (substantial original analysis per page, not templates).

### KS-2: Cold outreach to web agencies for backlinks (added 2026-04-19)
- **Refutation evidence:** 825 emails sent across multiple verticals and template variants. 0 responses.
- **Why it cannot be retried:** A 0% response rate at this volume implies a structural mismatch (offer, audience, channel, or sender reputation) that more sends will not fix.
- **What would have to change to retry:** A different offer entirely (e.g., paid placement, partnership equity, free tooling), tracked telemetry (opens/clicks), and pilot of 10 emails before any scale-up.

### KS-3: Mass Reddit / Quora posting for referral traffic (added 2026-04-19)
- **Refutation evidence:** Multiple posts across both platforms. No measurable traffic resulted.
- **Why it cannot be retried:** Both platforms now suppress link-bearing posts from low-karma accounts. The effort-to-result ratio is empirically near zero.
- **What would have to change to retry:** A real account with established standing in a specific subreddit, posting genuinely useful answers without immediate link drops.

## Killed tactics

- **Refutation evidence:** Submitted; merged in some; produced 0 measurable referral traffic.
- **Why it cannot be retried:** These lists no longer drive meaningful traffic; SEO weight from them is negligible.
