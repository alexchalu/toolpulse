# CalcLeap — Experiment Log

Append-only log of every experiment Rando ran on CalcLeap. Format: date, hypothesis tested, what was done, result, lesson.

## 2026-03-07 → 2026-04-19 (pre-rebuild — summary)

**Hypothesis:** Building 3,000+ calculator pages + sending mass outreach + Reddit posting will generate SEO traffic and AdSense + lead-gen revenue.

**What was done:**
- ~3,012 calculator pages built and pushed to alexchalu/toolpulse (calcleap.com)
- 825 outreach emails sent across web-agency, publisher, insurance, legal, real-estate, HVAC verticals
- Multiple Reddit and Quora posts
- Multiple directory submissions
- Multiple Dev.to articles
- AdSense application submitted

**Result:**
- 0 traffic (CalcLeap views in last 14d: 0)
- 0 unique visitors
- 5/3012 pages indexed by Google
- 0 outreach responses
- AdSense rejected ("low value content")
- $0 revenue

**Lesson:**
- Mass content production without solving the indexing problem is pure waste.
- Cold outreach with the current template has a 0% response rate at scale; either the template is wrong or the targeting is wrong, but doing more of it produces nothing.
- The bottleneck is upstream of all the actions taken: the site has no signal Google trusts (no backlinks from quality domains, no real engagement, no original brand). Without that, no amount of pages or outreach moves anything.
- **Strategic implication:** the next experiment must address the trust/distinctiveness problem, not the volume problem.
