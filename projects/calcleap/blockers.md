# CalcLeap — Blockers

Things actively preventing progress. Each blocker has a clear owner and a "what would unblock this."

## Active blockers

### B1: Google indexing (5/3012 pages)
- **Why it's blocking:** No traffic possible without indexing. Without traffic, no AdSense, no leads, no validation of any other hypothesis.
- **Likely root cause:** No quality backlinks signal trust; the domain is 27 days old; content is templated and lacks distinctiveness.
- **What would unblock:** Either (a) earn a few high-quality editorial backlinks from authoritative domains, or (b) abandon the SEO premise entirely and pursue a non-SEO revenue path.
- **Owner:** weekly-retro must decide direction.

### B2: AdSense rejection ("low value content")
- **Why it's blocking:** Even if pages were indexed, no ad revenue is possible until AdSense is approved.
- **Likely root cause:** Templated calculator pages with thin original content trip Google's "auto-generated content" filters.
- **What would unblock:** Either (a) reduce site to ~50–100 pages with substantial original analysis, then reapply, or (b) pursue lead-gen / SaaS revenue paths that don't depend on AdSense.
- **Owner:** weekly-retro must decide direction.

### B3: Outreach 0% response rate (0/825)
- **Why it's blocking:** Outreach is the current primary distribution strategy and it's empirically dead.
- **Likely root cause:** Either the template, the targeting, or both. We don't know because we have no telemetry on opens/clicks.
- **What would unblock:** (a) Stop sending. (b) Before resuming, A/B test 10 emails with new template + tracking, only scale if response rate > 2%.
- **Owner:** any future outreach must be gated through the `red-team` skill.
