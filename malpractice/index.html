Understanding Medical Malpractice Settlements

Medical malpractice occurs when a healthcare provider deviates from the accepted standard of care, resulting in patient injury. Settlement values depend on injury severity, medical costs, lost income, state laws, and available insurance coverage.

Key factors affecting your case value:

  • Severity and permanence of injury
  • Total medical expenses (past and future)
  • Lost wages and diminished earning capacity
  • State damage cap laws
  • Strength of evidence and expert testimony
  • Insurance policy limits of the provider
