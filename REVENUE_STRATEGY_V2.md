# CalcLeap Revenue Strategy V2 — Deep Analysis

## The Problem
We have 2,900+ pages and $0 revenue. Spray-and-pray outreach to generic emails won't work. We need a professional, high-quality approach.

## How NerdWallet Actually Makes Money
NerdWallet revenue (2023): $632M. Their model:
1. **Affiliate commissions** — Users click "Apply Now" on a credit card → NerdWallet gets $25-100 per approval
2. **Lead generation** — Insurance quotes, mortgage pre-approvals → $20-200 per qualified lead
3. **NOT AdSense** — AdSense is pennies. Affiliate + lead gen is the real money.

## The Strategic Pivot
CalcLeap should NOT rely on AdSense. We need:
1. **Financial affiliate links** on every calculator result page
2. **Lead capture forms** that sell to aggregators
3. **Comparison tables** for credit cards, insurance, mortgages

## Immediate Revenue Actions (Executable Now)

### 1. Sign up for affiliate networks (FREE, instant)
- **Impact Radius** — hosts Credit Karma, Quicken Loans
- **FlexOffers** — thousands of finance offers, low barrier
- **ShareASale** — Lending Tree, insurance companies  
- **Bankrate affiliate** — $25-100 per credit card application

### 2. Add comparison tables to top calculator pages
- Mortgage calculator → "Compare mortgage rates" table with affiliate links
- Insurance calculators → "Get quotes from top insurers" 
- Tax calculators → "File with TurboTax" affiliate link

### 3. Create "Best X" comparison pages (highest revenue per page)
- Best credit cards 2026 (each click = $25-100)
- Best savings accounts 2026 (each signup = $10-50)
- Best mortgage lenders 2026 (each lead = $50-200)
- Best insurance companies 2026 (each quote = $20-100)

## Quality Standard
Every page must look like it belongs on NerdWallet or Bankrate.
- Professional comparison tables
- Trust badges and security indicators
- Editorial-quality content
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast load times
