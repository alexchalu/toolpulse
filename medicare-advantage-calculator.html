Understanding Medicare

When planning for healthcare costs, it's important to understand:

  • Monthly premium costs and how they vary by age
  • Out-of-pocket maximums and deductibles
  • Coverage levels and what they include
  • Long-term cost implications of different plans

When to Review Your Coverage

  • During annual enrollment periods
  • When experiencing major life changes
  • If your health needs change significantly
  • Before making major healthcare decisions
