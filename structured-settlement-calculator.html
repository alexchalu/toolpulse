💡 What is a Structured Settlement?

A structured settlement is a financial arrangement where a claimant receives periodic payments over time rather than a lump sum. These are commonly used in personal injury lawsuits, workers' compensation claims, and other legal settlements.

📊 Why Calculate Present Value?

Present value calculations help you understand how much your future payments are worth today. This is crucial for:

  • Comparing lump sum offers
  • Making financial decisions
  • Understanding your settlement's true value
  • Negotiating with insurance companies
