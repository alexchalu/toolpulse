How California Income Tax is Calculated

California's income tax system is progressive, meaning higher earners pay a larger percentage of their income in taxes. The state uses several tax brackets and allows for various deductions and credits. Our calculator provides an estimate based on your gross income, filing status, and number of dependents.

Factors like specific deductions, tax credits (e.g., Earned Income Tax Credit, Child and Dependent Care Credit), and other income sources (e.g., capital gains, rental income) can affect your actual tax liability. This tool is for estimation purposes only and should not be considered tax advice. Consult a qualified tax professional for personalized guidance.
