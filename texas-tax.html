❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Texas income tax rate in 2026?

Texas does not levy a state income tax. Residents keep 100% of their earned income from state taxation.

What is the Texas sales tax rate?

The Texas state sales tax rate is 6.25%. However, cities and counties may add local sales taxes, so the total rate you pay could be higher. Always check the combined rate for your specific location.

How much is property tax in Texas?

The average effective property tax rate in Texas is 1.80% of your home's assessed value. For a $350,000 home, that's approximately $6,300 per year. Actual rates vary by county and municipality.

Is Texas a tax-friendly state?

Texas is very tax-friendly — it has no state income tax! However, the state may offset this with a 6.25% sales tax rate and above-average property taxes at 1.80%.
