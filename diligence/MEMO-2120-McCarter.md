## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

2120 McCarter Highway is an approximately **6.49-acre** industrial outdoor storage site located in Newark, Essex County, New Jersey. The property is **100% leased to Amazon.com Services LLC** under a long-term parking lease executed June 9, 2021, with a **5-year initial term** (60 months from commencement). The lease is structured as an **absolute net lease** with 3% annual escalations on controllable operating expenses.

The property is strategically positioned with direct access to **Port Newark/Port Elizabeth** (one of the largest container ports on the East Coast), **Newark Liberty International Airport**, and the **NJ Turnpike/I-95 corridor**. Amazon utilizes the site as **EWR8_P2** — a long-term parking facility supporting their logistics operations.

**Key Investment Highlights:**
- Investment-grade tenant (Amazon, S&P AA)
- Absolute NNN lease structure
- ~$1.56M Year 1 base rent with 3% annual escalation cap
- Total term base rent: $8,269,510
- Strategic port/airport-adjacent location in Northern NJ
- IOS asset class experiencing 123% rent growth since 2020

## RISKS & CONCERNS

1. **Environmental liability** — Methane venting, deed notice, and adjacent property demolition suggest brownfield conditions. Full Phase I ESA review critical.
2. **Lease duration** — 5-year term is relatively short for industrial; approaching or past mid-term depending on actual commencement date. **Renewal terms not visible in extracted pages — critical to verify.**
3. **Waterway encroachment** — Lot 18 borders Second River/Passaic River with no title insurance to riverbed land. Flood risk.
4. **NJ Route 21 access restrictions** — State retains right to limit ingress/egress, which could impair property value.
5. **Single-tenant risk** — 100% reliance on Amazon. Vacancy would eliminate all income.
6. **Private lender** — Existing $12.375M mortgage held by private lender (ARESIF Lender LLC). Assumption/defeasance terms unknown.
7. **Adjacent redevelopment** — 2104-2118 McCarter Hwy under demolition. Future use could be beneficial (complementary industrial) or detrimental (conflicting use).

## RECOMMENDATION

**Proceed with caution.** The property offers compelling fundamentals — Amazon credit, NNN structure, and a prime Northern NJ IOS location in a market with exceptional rent growth. The $19,999/acre/month rent is strong and the 3% escalation provides built-in NOI growth.

**Key diligence items before commitment:**
1. **Full Phase I ESA review** — 51.8 MB document needs complete analysis. Methane + deed notice suggest material environmental conditions.
2. **Lease renewal/extension terms** — If the 5-year term started in late 2021, it may expire as early as late 2026. Are there renewal options? What are the terms?
3. **Amazon operational plans** — Is EWR8_P2 still active? Has Amazon expressed renewal intent?
4. **Existing mortgage disposition** — $12.375M loan from ARESIF must be addressed. Assumption, payoff, or defeasance?
5. **Flood zone analysis** — Given proximity to Second River/Passaic River, FEMA flood zone designation is critical.
6. **Survey review** — ALTA survey on file should confirm exact acreage, easements, and encroachments.

**Pricing guidance:** At a 5.5-6.0% cap rate on current NOI, the property values at approximately **$26-28M**. However, if the lease is near expiration without renewal options, significant re-leasing risk may warrant a higher cap rate (6.5-7.0%), reducing value to **$22-24M**.
