## 7. Zoning — Kenilworth Borough "I" (Industrial) District

**Source:** Kenilworth Borough Code, Chapter 120 Land Use, Part 3 Zoning, Article 22 (eCode360 §31837267)

### Purpose
The I District is "designed to protect and concentrate those commercial and industrial/manufacturing uses that are currently viable and those sites for which industrial/commercial reuse is feasible and probable." Also designed for uses with "vehicular traffic or utilitarian or operational requirements" more suitable near major arterials than residential areas.

### Permitted Uses (§120-22.14)
- Manufacturing, processing, fabrication
- Warehousing and distribution
- Research & development (R&D)
- Office uses
- Outdoor storage (with conditions)
- Transportation and logistics
- Various commercial/industrial uses with performance standards

### Overlay Note
- The area between North 10th and North 12th Streets (between Washington and Monroe Avenues) has a **Senior Citizens Housing Overlay Zone** allowing age-restricted (55+) and disabled housing.

### Route 22 Corridor Context
- Property is in the Route 78/22 East submarket, one of the core industrial corridors in Northern NJ
- Excellent highway access: Route 22 (directly adjacent), Route 78 (nearby), Garden State Parkway
- 20 miles from NYC; 11 miles from Newark Liberty International Airport
- 2.5 miles from Cranford NJ Transit station (Raritan Valley Line)
- 10 miles from Port Newark/Elizabeth
