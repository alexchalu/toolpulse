## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

76 Central Avenue is an approximately **9.749-acre** (424,655 SF) industrial outdoor storage site located in the South Kearny section of Kearny, Hudson County, New Jersey. The property is leased to **Port Kearny Security, Inc.** under a lease dated August 19, 2021 with a **122-month term** (~10.2 years) for truck/trailer storage and container operations.

⚠️ **CRITICAL RED FLAG:** The tenant fell into significant arrears of **$414,456.80** in unpaid rent, necessitating a Fourth Amendment (January 8, 2025) that granted a **rent abatement for the remainder of the term** in exchange for a partial catch-up payment and installment plan. This dramatically impairs the property's near-term income profile.

The property is zoned **SKI-S (South Kearny Industrial South)** — one of the most favorable IOS zonings in the New Jersey market. Four **Recognized Environmental Conditions (RECs)** and one **Controlled Recognized Environmental Condition (CREC)** were identified in the Phase I ESA.

**Key Investment Highlights:**
- 9.75 acres in South Kearny — prime IOS location near NJ Turnpike
- SKI-S zoning permits truck/trailer storage as of right (legal conforming)
- IOS land values in Northern NJ: $2-3M+/acre for properly zoned sites
- Significant upside if tenant credit is stabilized or property re-leased at market

**Key Concerns:**
- Tenant in arrears ($414K); rent abated through lease expiration
- 4 RECs + 1 CREC in Phase I ESA (historic fill, railroad contamination, petroleum staining, groundwater CEA)
- Single-tenant risk with distressed tenant

## RECOMMENDATION

**HIGH-RISK / HIGH-REWARD OPPORTUNITY.** This property has compelling long-term fundamentals but requires significant near-term risk management.

### Bull Case
- Acquire at land value ($5-10M) given zero current NOI
- Exercise Landlord termination right or wait for lease expiration
- Re-lease to creditworthy IOS tenant at market rates ($1.87-2.6M/year)
- Stabilized value: $30-40M at 5.5-6.5% cap rate
- **Potential 3-4x return** on cost if environmental is manageable

### Bear Case
- Environmental remediation costs $2-5M+ (Phase II could reveal worse conditions)
- Groundwater CEA expands; institutional controls limit site use
- Re-leasing takes 12-18 months with tenant improvement obligations
- Port Kearny Security abandons site leaving cleanup obligations

### Key Diligence Priorities
1. **Phase II Environmental** — Sub-surface investigation for all 4 RECs. Critical.
2. **Groundwater sampling** — Determine if CEA from adjacent property has impacted site groundwater
3. **Landlord termination right** — Review Article 2 terms. Can Landlord terminate early and re-lease?
4. **Port Kearny Security financial status** — Is the tenant solvent? Will they honor the installment plan?
5. **NJ DEP records** — Full history of site from NJDEP DataMiner
6. **Tax assessment** — Current and projected real estate taxes on 9.75 acres
7. **Existing mortgage** — Any debt on the property? Title search for liens.

### Pricing Guidance
- **As-Is (distressed):** $5.0-8.0M ($500K-820K/acre) — land value with environmental discount
- **Stabilized (re-leased):** $30-40M at 5.5-6.5% cap on $1.87-2.6M NOI
- **Appropriate discount for environmental risk and re-leasing time:** 40-60% off stabilized value
- **Target acquisition price:** $12-18M (reflects environmental risk premium and re-leasing timeline)
