## 2. INVESTMENT THESIS & STRATEGIC RATIONALE

### IOS Asset Class Thesis

Industrial Outdoor Storage (IOS) has emerged as one of the most compelling net lease sub-sectors in commercial real estate, driven by:

- **Supply-Demand Imbalance:** IOS-zoned land is inherently scarce in urban infill markets, with municipalities increasingly restricting new IOS development through zoning and environmental regulations. The constrained supply pipeline contrasts with accelerating demand from e-commerce logistics, last-mile delivery, construction, and infrastructure sectors.
- **Rent Growth Trajectory:** National IOS rents have experienced unprecedented growth, with the Northern NJ market seeing a 123% increase in IOS rents per SF since 2020 (Yardi Matrix, October 2025). The national average IOS rent stands at $6,511/acre/month (Matthews Real Estate Investment Services, Q1 2024), while NJ/NY metro markets command a significant premium of $8,000–$15,000+/acre/month.
- **Institutional Capital Flows:** Institutional investors including Blackstone, Brookfield, and specialized IOS platforms have aggressively allocated capital to the sector, compressing cap rates and establishing IOS as a core real estate allocation.
- **Operational Simplicity:** Unlike traditional industrial warehousing, IOS assets require minimal capital expenditure, no building systems maintenance, and limited management intensity — producing attractive risk-adjusted returns on an absolute net basis.

### Location Thesis

The Newark/Northern NJ logistics corridor is among the most supply-constrained and demand-intensive industrial markets in the United States:

- **Port Newark/Port Elizabeth:** One of the largest container ports on the U.S. East Coast, handling over 7 million TEUs annually, located approximately 5 miles (10–15 minute drive) from the property.
- **Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR):** Major air cargo hub approximately 4 miles (10 minute drive) from the property, a critical node in Amazon's air logistics network.
- **NJ Turnpike / I-95 Corridor:** Approximately 2 miles from the property, providing access to the entire Northeast corridor from Maine to Virginia.
- **Route 21 (McCarter Highway) Direct Frontage:** The property enjoys direct access to NJ Route 21, a major north-south arterial connecting Newark's industrial core to the broader highway network.
- **Adjacent to Former Lackawanna Railroad ROW:** Historical rail infrastructure (now Conrail) borders the property to the south, reflecting the area's deep logistics heritage.
- **Passaic River / Second River Confluence:** While presenting flood risk, the waterway proximity reinforces the industrial character of the immediate area and creates natural barriers to residential encroachment.

### Amazon Platform Thesis

Amazon.com Services LLC represents the highest-quality credit tenant available in the IOS/logistics market:

- **Credit Quality:** Amazon.com, Inc. carries an S&P rating of AA and Moody's rating of A1, reflecting its dominant market position, massive free cash flow generation, and fortress balance sheet.
- **Essential Infrastructure:** The EWR8 delivery station is a core node in Amazon's Northern NJ last-mile network. EWR8_P2 provides dedicated parking for the delivery vehicle fleet, a function that cannot be easily relocated without disruption to Amazon's service levels.
- **Scale and Stickiness:** Amazon's logistics infrastructure investments create high switching costs. The cost of relocating, re-permitting, and re-configuring a parking facility in the supply-constrained Northern NJ market significantly exceeds the rent obligations under this lease.
- **Growth Trajectory:** Amazon continues to expand its logistics footprint nationally, with particular emphasis on dense urban markets that require last-mile infrastructure.

## 15. INVESTMENT RECOMMENDATION

### Summary Assessment

2120 McCarter Highway is a well-located, institutionally-tenanted IOS asset in one of the nation's strongest industrial markets. The Amazon credit, absolute NNN structure, and Northern NJ logistics location create a compelling investment profile. However, the investment carries material risk factors that must be addressed before proceeding:

### Strengths
1. **Amazon.com, Inc. guaranty** (S&P: AA) — among the strongest tenant credits available in any real estate sector
2. **Absolute NNN structure** with 3% annual escalations and 103% controllable cap — predictable, growing income with minimal landlord burden
3. **Premium IOS location** in supply-constrained Northern NJ market with 123% rent growth since 2020
4. **Comprehensive environmental protections** in lease — all pre-existing liability, methane monitoring, deed notice compliance, and toxic dust mitigation assigned to Landlord
5. **I-2 zoning with 96-foot height allowance** provides significant development optionality in reversion
6. **$19,000–$20,000/acre/month rent** is strong but defensible given location and credit quality
7. **156 truck parking spaces** on improved, paved, lit, fenced site — immediate utility for logistics operators

### Weaknesses
1. **No renewal options** and potentially limited remaining term (possibly expiring late 2026)
2. **Extensive environmental history** — 130+ years of industrial use, 5 RECs, Deed Notice, methane, historic fill
3. **Complex title** with 19 exceptions, state access restrictions, riverbed exclusions, and pending Riparian/Tidelands search
4. **Flood zone exposure** on Lot 18 (15% of parking capacity)
5. **Private lender mortgage** with non-standard satisfaction requirements
6. **Single point of vehicular access** from McCarter Highway (state-controlled)

### Pricing Guidance

| Scenario | Cap Rate | Value Range | Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Premium** (confirmed renewal/extension) | 5.0%–5.5% | $28.3M–$31.2M | Justified only with confirmed Amazon renewal for 5+ years |
| **Market** (short remaining term, uncertain renewal) | 6.0%–6.5% | $24.0M–$26.0M | Appropriate for current conditions with re-leasing risk |
| **Discount** (environmental concerns, term expiration imminent) | 7.0%–7.5% | $20.8M–$22.3M | Required if environmental cleanup costs are uncertain or term is within 12 months of expiration |

### Recommendation

**PROCEED WITH CAUTION — Conditional on resolution of the following:**

1. **Lease Term Verification:** Confirm exact Commencement Date. If the lease expires within 18 months, pricing must reflect significant re-leasing risk (7.0%+ cap rate) unless Amazon renewal intent is confirmed.

2. **Amazon Engagement:** Obtain an estoppel certificate or direct indication of renewal intent from Amazon. An Amazon commitment to extend would materially increase value by $5M–$8M.

3. **Environmental Closure:** Verify that NJDEP PI 019658 has received closure documentation (NFA letter and Confirmed Discharge Notification) as required by Exhibit C. If closure has not been achieved, quantify remaining remediation costs and timeline.
