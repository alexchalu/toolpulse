## Recommendation

**Investment Verdict: CONDITIONAL BUY — Pricing Must Reflect Near-Term Lease Expiration**

The investment thesis for 2120 McCarter Highway is fundamentally a **land value play** with optionality. The Amazon lease expires in approximately 2-3 months (June 2026), converting this from an income-producing asset to a land holding. The property's value derives from:

1. **Location Premium:** Port Newark adjacency, Route 21 frontage, and established IOS zoning create persistent demand from logistics, trucking, and e-commerce operators.

2. **Sector Tailwinds:** IOS remains a supply-constrained asset class. Criterion's own portfolio growth (22 to 34 properties in 2 years) and the Harrison/Kearny IOS portfolio sale at $3.21M/acre (Oct 2024) evidence institutional demand.

3. **Amazon Relationship:** Even post-lease, Amazon's prior occupancy validates the site for IOS use. Lease extension negotiations or re-leasing to other logistics operators is a viable path.

**Pricing Guidance:**
- **If Amazon renews:** Stabilized NOI of $1.55M-$1.75M at 6.5%-7.0% cap = $22M-$27M
- **If vacant post-June 2026:** IOS land value of $5M-$15M (midpoint ~$10M) based on comps
- **Downside protection:** Axos Bank's willingness to refinance at portfolio level suggests institutional comfort with asset quality

**Required Pre-Acquisition Actions:**
1. Confirm Amazon lease extension status — this is the single most important variable
2. Conduct GPR survey for undocumented USTs ($5K-$10K cost; critical for liability assessment)
3. Verify Flood Zone AE implications on insurance costs and development potential
4. Obtain current Essex County tax assessment (unavailable in research)
5. Reconcile lot size discrepancy (4.83 acres per tax records vs. 6.5-6.9 acres in marketing)
6. Review full ALTA survey for easements, encroachments, and access constraints
7. Request methane vent system maintenance records and LSRP status update

**Pass/Fail Criteria:**
- PASS if Amazon renews at or above current rent and acquisition price reflects stabilized value
- PASS if vacant pricing is ≤$10M (supported by land comps)
- FAIL if asking price assumes stabilized income without lease extension in hand
- FAIL if GPR survey reveals extensive UST contamination requiring >$500K remediation
