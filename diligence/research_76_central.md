## 13. Summary Assessment

**The $4.8M asking price is a major anomaly.** Criterion Group purchased this 8.07-acre industrial site for $13.75M in February 2021. Comparable IOS land in the Harrison/Kearny area traded at ~$3.21M/acre in October 2024. At that rate, this site would be worth **$25.9M** — more than 5x the reported $4.8M asking.

**Most likely explanations:**
1. The $4.8M is for a **subdivided smaller parcel** (perhaps the 5.05 AC referenced in LoopNet)
2. The property has **significant environmental liabilities** reducing value
3. The figure is a **lease value** (perhaps annual or multi-year lease total), not a sale price
4. The source of the $4.8M figure needs verification

**If genuinely available for $4.8M for 5+ acres of industrially zoned IOS land in South Kearny, NJ, this would represent an extraordinary below-market opportunity** — warranting immediate verification and aggressive pursuit, subject to environmental and flood risk clearance.
