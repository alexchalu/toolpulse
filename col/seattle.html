About Living in Seattle

Cost of Living Index: 138 (national average = 100). Seattle is 38% more expensive than the US average.

Housing: The biggest expense in Seattle. Average 1BR apartment costs $2,300/month.

Transportation: Good public transit system. Monthly pass costs $99.

Seattle Living Costs Breakdown (2026)

  • Studio apartment: $1,800/month
  • 1 bedroom: $2,300/month
  • Groceries (1 person): $430/month
  • Transit pass: $99/month
  • Utilities: $125/month (electric, gas, water)

Tips for Living in Seattle

  • Consider neighborhoods outside downtown for cheaper rent (20-40% savings)
  • Use public transit when possible - cheaper than car ownership
  • Cook at home - eating out costs 3-4x more than groceries
  • Look for roommates to split housing costs
