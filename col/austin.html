About Living in Austin

Cost of Living Index: 110 (national average = 100). Austin is 10% more expensive than the US average.

Housing: The biggest expense in Austin. Average 1BR apartment costs $1,800/month.

Transportation: Good public transit system. Monthly pass costs $80.

Austin Living Costs Breakdown (2026)

  • Studio apartment: $1,400/month
  • 1 bedroom: $1,800/month
  • Groceries (1 person): $360/month
  • Transit pass: $80/month
  • Utilities: $140/month (electric, gas, water)

Tips for Living in Austin

  • Consider neighborhoods outside downtown for cheaper rent (20-40% savings)
  • Use public transit when possible - cheaper than car ownership
  • Cook at home - eating out costs 3-4x more than groceries
  • Look for roommates to split housing costs
