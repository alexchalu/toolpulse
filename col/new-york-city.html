About Living in New York City

Cost of Living Index: 187 (national average = 100). New York City is 87% more expensive than the US average.

Housing: The biggest expense in New York City. Average 1BR apartment costs $4,100/month.

Transportation: Car recommended. Budget for gas, insurance, and parking.

New York City Living Costs Breakdown (2026)

  • Studio apartment: $3,200/month
  • 1 bedroom: $4,100/month
  • Groceries (1 person): $450/month
  • Transit pass: $127/month
  • Utilities: $140/month (electric, gas, water)

Tips for Living in New York City

  • Consider neighborhoods outside downtown for cheaper rent (20-40% savings)
  • Use public transit when possible - cheaper than car ownership
  • Cook at home - eating out costs 3-4x more than groceries
  • Look for roommates to split housing costs
