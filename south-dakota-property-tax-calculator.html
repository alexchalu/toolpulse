About South Dakota Property Taxes

The effective property tax rate in South Dakota is 1.31% of the home's assessed value. This means for every $100,000 in home value, you'll pay approximately $1310 in annual property taxes.

South Dakota ranks in the middle range for property tax rates in the United States.
