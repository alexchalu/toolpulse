Mesothelioma Compensation Calculator

Estimate potential compensation for mesothelioma claims based on diagnosis type, exposure history, state laws, and case factors.

⚠️ Important Legal Disclaimer: This calculator provides rough estimates only based on typical settlement ranges. Actual compensation varies dramatically based on defendant solvency, jurisdiction, attorney quality, case strength, jury composition, and many other unpredictable factors. About Mesothelioma Compensation

Mesothelioma compensation typically comes from three sources: asbestos trust funds (over $30 billion available), lawsuit settlements, and trial verdicts. Average settlements range from $1 million to $2.4 million, though amounts vary widely based on case specifics.

Factors Affecting Compensation

  • Diagnosis type and stage: Earlier diagnosis and less common types may receive higher awards
  • Exposure history: Longer exposure periods and clearer liability chains increase value
  • Age and dependents: Younger victims with families receive higher compensation for lost income
  • State laws: Some states have more favorable statutes of limitations and damage caps
  • Medical costs: Higher treatment expenses increase economic damages
  • Defendant solvency: Cases against bankrupt companies rely on trust funds with set payout schedules

Types of Compensation Available

  • Medical expenses: Past and future treatment costs, including surgery, chemotherapy, radiation
  • Lost income: Past lost wages and future earning capacity
  • Pain and suffering: Non-economic damages for physical and emotional distress
  • Punitive damages: Additional awards in cases of gross negligence (rare)
  • Wrongful death: Compensation for surviving family members

Timeline and Process

Mesothelioma claims typically take 12-18 months to settle, though expedited processes exist for terminal cases. Most cases (95%+) settle out of court. Attorney fees are typically 33-40% of recovery on contingency (no upfront cost).
