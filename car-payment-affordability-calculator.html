How It Works

This calculator helps you make informed decisions about your vehicle purchase by providing accurate cost estimates based on:

  • Vehicle purchase price and down payment
  • Interest rate and loan term
  • Total cost of financing
  • Monthly payment breakdown

Tips for Auto Affordability

  • Compare multiple financing options before deciding
  • Consider the total cost of ownership, not just monthly payments
  • Factor in insurance, maintenance, and fuel costs
  • Shop around for the best interest rates
