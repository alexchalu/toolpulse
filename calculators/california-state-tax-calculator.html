About California State Taxes

California has one of the highest state income tax rates in the United States. The state uses a progressive tax system with 9 tax brackets ranging from 1% to 12.3%. In addition to state income tax, California residents also pay Federal income tax and FICA taxes (Social Security and Medicare).

Important Notes

  • These calculations are estimates for tax year 2024
  • Actual tax liability may vary based on deductions, credits, and other factors
  • California does not allow itemized deductions on state return
  • Standard deduction amounts vary by filing status
  • Consider consulting a tax professional for personalized advice
