**Target Market:**
- Local businesses with $500K-$10M revenue
- Team size: 5-50 employees
- High lead volume (50+ inquiries/month)
- Already using CRM/booking software (tech-ready)

**Key Industries:**
1. Healthcare (dental, medical practices)
2. Real Estate (agencies, brokerages)
3. Insurance (brokers, agents)
4. Legal (law firms, solo practitioners scaling up)
5. Home Services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical)

**Differentiation:**
- Not a SaaS chatbot vendor → Custom AI development firm
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure → Not templates or plugins
- Focus on ROI → Measurable revenue impact, not vanity metrics
- White-glove service → We handle complexity, not DIY

**Pricing Psychology:**
- "Investment" not "cost"
- "Starting at" creates room for upsells
- Monthly retainer = recurring revenue
- $2,500 setup filters out tire-kickers
- ROI messaging: Most clients see payback in 30-60 days
