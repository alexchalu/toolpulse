Understanding Mega Backdoor Roth

Key factors that impact your retirement savings:

  • Regular contributions compound over time (time is your biggest advantage)
  • Expected returns vary based on asset allocation and risk tolerance

Maximizing Your Savings

  • Increase contributions when you get raises
  • Diversify investments to manage risk
  • Review and rebalance portfolio annually
