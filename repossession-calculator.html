Understanding Repossession

When dealing with debt challenges, it's important to understand your options:

  • Bankruptcy provides legal protection but impacts credit for 7-10 years
  • Debt settlement can reduce what you owe but may have tax consequences
  • Consolidation simplifies payments but doesn't reduce total debt
  • Credit counseling offers manageable payment plans with creditor cooperation

Getting Help

  • Consult with a bankruptcy attorney for personalized legal advice
  • Contact nonprofit credit counseling agencies (NFCC certified)
  • Understand your rights under the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act
  • Explore all options before making major financial decisions
