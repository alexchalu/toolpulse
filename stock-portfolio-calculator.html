About the Stock Portfolio Calculator

This calculator helps you estimate the future value of your stock portfolio based on your investment strategy. It accounts for your initial investment, regular contributions, expected returns, and asset allocation across stocks, bonds, and cash.

How It Works

  1. Enter your investment details: Initial investment amount, monthly contribution, and investment period.
  2. Set your expected return: Based on historical market averages (typically 7-10% for stocks).
  3. Define your asset allocation: Adjust the percentage allocated to stocks, bonds, and cash.
  4. Calculate your results: See your final portfolio value, total contributions, and returns.

Key Features

  • Real-time calculation of portfolio growth
  • Asset allocation breakdown
  • Total contributions vs. investment returns
  • Annualized return calculation
  • Mobile-friendly design

Investment Tips

  • Diversify your portfolio: Don't put all your money in one asset class.
  • Invest consistently: Regular contributions compound over time.
  • Consider your risk tolerance: Younger investors can typically take more risk.
  • Review periodically: Rebalance your portfolio as needed.
