How the Loan Comparison Calculator Works

Our Loan Comparison Calculator helps you make informed financial decisions by breaking down the costs and payments of up to three different loans. Simply enter the principal amount, annual interest rate, and loan term for each loan you're considering. The calculator will then compute and display the estimated monthly payment, total interest paid, and the total cost of each loan.

Key Metrics Explained:

  • Monthly Payment: The amount you will pay each month towards the principal and interest.
  • Total Interest Paid: The cumulative interest you will pay over the entire term of the loan.
  • Total Cost of Loan: The sum of the principal amount and the total interest paid.

By comparing these key metrics, you can quickly identify which loan offers the most favorable terms for your financial situation, whether you're looking for the lowest monthly payment, the least total interest, or the lowest overall cost.
