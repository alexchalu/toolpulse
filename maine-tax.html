❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Maine income tax rate in 2026?

Maine has a state income tax rate of 5.8-7.15% with 3 brackets. Graduated income tax.

What is the Maine sales tax rate?

The Maine state sales tax rate is 5.5%. However, cities and counties may add local sales taxes, so the total rate you pay could be higher. Always check the combined rate for your specific location.

How much is property tax in Maine?

The average effective property tax rate in Maine is 1.36% of your home's assessed value. For a $350,000 home, that's approximately $4,760 per year. Actual rates vary by county and municipality.

Is Maine a tax-friendly state?

Maine is considered a higher-tax state with income tax rates up to 5.8-7.15%. However, the overall tax burden depends on sales tax, property tax, and cost of living.
