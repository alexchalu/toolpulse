How New York State Income Tax is Calculated

New York State income tax is progressive, meaning higher earners pay a larger percentage of their income in taxes. The calculation involves:

  • Gross Income: Your total income before deductions.
  • Adjusted Gross Income (AGI): Gross income minus certain deductions.
  • Standard or Itemized Deductions: Reduces your taxable income.
  • Tax Brackets: New York has several tax brackets with varying rates.
  • Credits: Various credits can reduce your tax liability directly.

This calculator provides an estimate and should not be considered tax advice. Consult a tax professional for personalized guidance.
