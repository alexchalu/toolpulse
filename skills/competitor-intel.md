When doing competitor research:

1. Identify top competitors for a given niche/keyword
2. Use web_search and browser to analyze:
   - Their content strategy (what pages rank)
   - Their monetization (ads, leads, affiliate)
   - Their strengths and weaknesses
3. Find gaps where CalcLeap can win
4. Output: structured report with actionable items
5. Add best opportunities to memory/revenue-ideas.md
