# Revenue Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Generate creative revenue ideas and evaluate their potential. Think beyond the obvious.

## Trigger
When Rando needs to brainstorm or evaluate revenue opportunities.

## Process
1. Analyze current assets (CalcLeap, API, content, email list, skills)
2. Research what similar sites/tools monetize successfully
3. Generate 5 creative revenue ideas ranked by: ease of implementation, revenue potential, time to first dollar
4. For the top idea: create an action plan with specific steps
5. Execute the first step immediately

## Revenue Levers to Consider
- **Affiliate**: High-CPC verticals (insurance, legal, finance, real estate)
- **Lead gen**: Sell qualified leads to businesses
- **Premium features**: PDF export, saved calculations, comparison reports
- **API**: Monetize calculator APIs for developers
- **White-label**: Sell embeddable widgets to businesses
- **Content**: Sponsored posts, paid reviews, comparison pages
- **Data**: Compile and sell industry data/benchmarks
- **Consulting**: Use tools as lead magnets for consulting inquiries
- **Micro-SaaS**: Small recurring-revenue tools built on calculator infrastructure

## Output Format
For each idea:
- **What**: One-line description
- **Revenue model**: How it makes money
- **Effort**: Low/Medium/High
- **Time to first *: Days/Weeks/Months
- **Expected monthly revenue**: Range estimate
- **First step**: Concrete action to take now
