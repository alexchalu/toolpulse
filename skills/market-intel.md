# Market Intelligence Skill

## Purpose
Research markets, competitors, and opportunities to inform Alex's business decisions.

## Trigger
When Alex asks about markets, competitors, or opportunities, or during heartbeat creative exploration.

## Process
1. Identify the specific market or question
2. Use web search to gather current data (multiple sources)
3. Look for primary data (official reports, SEC filings, pricing pages)
4. Analyze competitor positioning, pricing, and gaps
5. Quantify the opportunity (market size, growth, margins)
6. Present findings with clear recommendation

## Research Standards
- Multiple sources (never trust one)
- Primary data over secondary
- Quantify everything (sizes, rates, timelines, costs)
- Identify who benefits and who loses
- Note what you don't know
- Structured, scannable format
