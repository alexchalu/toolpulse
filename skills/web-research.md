When asked to research a topic:

1. Use web_search to find 5-10 relevant sources
2. Use web_fetch on the top 3-5 results to get full content
3. Cross-reference facts across multiple sources
4. Structure findings as:
   - Executive summary (3-5 bullets)
   - Key findings with sources
   - Data points and numbers
   - Actionable recommendations
5. Save research to memory/ files for future reference

Always cite sources. Prefer primary data over summaries.
