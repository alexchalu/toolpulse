How California Income Tax is Calculated

California has a progressive income tax system, meaning higher earners pay a larger percentage of their income in taxes. The tax rates range from 1% to 12.3%, with an additional 1% mental health services tax on income over $1,000,000. This calculator provides an estimate based on simplified tax brackets and common deductions/credits. For precise figures, consult a tax professional or official FTB resources.

Key Factors:

  • Gross Income: Your total income before deductions.
  • Filing Status: Single, Married Filing Jointly, Married Filing Separately, Head of Household, or Qualifying Widow(er).
  • Deductions: Standard deduction or itemized deductions reduce your taxable income.
  • Exemptions & Credits: Reduce the amount of tax you owe directly.
