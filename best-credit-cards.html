How We Choose the Best Credit Cards

Our editorial team evaluates 200+ credit cards across key metrics including rewards rates, sign-up bonuses, annual fees, APRs, and additional perks. We weight each factor based on its importance to the average consumer:

  • Rewards value (30%): Total cash back or points earned over 2 years of typical spending
  • Sign-up bonus (25%): Value of the welcome offer relative to spending requirements
  • Fees (20%): Annual fee, foreign transaction fees, balance transfer fees
  • APR terms (15%): Intro APR offers and regular APR competitiveness
  • Additional perks (10%): Travel insurance, purchase protection, airport lounge access
