About Recipe Scaling Calculator

This free recipe scaling calculator helps you adjust ingredient quantities when cooking for more or fewer people. Perfect for meal prep, batch cooking, and feeding crowds.

How to Use:

  • Enter your original recipe's serving size
  • Enter how many servings you want to make
  • Add your ingredients with amounts and units
  • Click "Scale Recipe" to see adjusted quantities

Common Scaling Scenarios:

  • Halving: Recipe serves 8, you need 4 portions
  • Doubling: Recipe serves 4, you need 8 portions (meal prep)
  • Tripling: Recipe serves 6, you need 18 portions (party)
  • Custom: Recipe serves 4, you need 12 portions (3x)
