Understanding Life Insurance Costs

Life insurance premiums vary significantly by state, age, health, and coverage type. Term life insurance is the most affordable option, typically costing $25-35/month for a healthy 35-year-old with $500,000 in coverage. Whole life insurance provides permanent coverage but costs 5-10x more.

Key factors that determine your rate:

  • Age: Every year you wait increases premiums by ~8-10%
  • Health: Excellent health can save you 20-40% on premiums
  • Tobacco use: Smokers pay 2-3x more than non-smokers
  • Coverage amount: More coverage costs more, but per-unit cost decreases
  • State regulations: Some states have rate restrictions that affect pricing
