❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the South Dakota income tax rate in 2026?

South Dakota does not levy a state income tax. Residents keep 100% of their earned income from state taxation.

What is the South Dakota sales tax rate?

The South Dakota state sales tax rate is 4.5%. However, cities and counties may add local sales taxes, so the total rate you pay could be higher. Always check the combined rate for your specific location.

How much is property tax in South Dakota?

The average effective property tax rate in South Dakota is 1.28% of your home's assessed value. For a $350,000 home, that's approximately $4,480 per year. Actual rates vary by county and municipality.

Is South Dakota a tax-friendly state?

South Dakota is very tax-friendly — it has no state income tax! However, the state may offset this with a 4.5% sales tax rate and above-average property taxes at 1.28%.
