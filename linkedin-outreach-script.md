# LinkedIn Outreach Script — Franklin Lakes, NJ Business Owners

## Target Profile
- **Location:** Franklin Lakes, NJ + surrounding areas (Wyckoff, Oakland, Ridgewood, Ramsey)
- **Title:** Owner, CEO, Managing Partner, Founder
- **Industry:** Dental, Law, Real Estate, Insurance, Home Services, Healthcare
- **Company size:** 5-50 employees

## Connection Request Message (300 char limit)

### Version 1: Direct Value
> Hi [First Name], I help [Industry] businesses in Franklin Lakes capture 90%+ of leads (vs the typical 60%). Noticed your practice/firm on Google. Mind if I connect?

### Version 2: Local Angle
> Hi [First Name], fellow Franklin Lakes local here. I build AI automation for [Industry] businesses. Would love to connect and share what's working locally.

### Version 3: Curiosity
> Hi [First Name], quick question: do you know how many leads [Company] is losing to slow response times? I help local businesses fix this. Connect?

## Follow-up Message (After Connection Accepted)

### Message 1: Value First (Send within 24 hours)

> Hey [First Name],
> 
> Thanks for connecting!
> 
> Quick question: How long does it typically take your team to respond to a new inquiry that comes in via your website or phone?
> 
> I ask because I work with [Industry] businesses around Franklin Lakes, and most are losing 20-30% of potential customers simply due to response time delays.
> 
> One dental practice I worked with went from a 4-hour average response time to under 30 seconds with AI automation — and added $42K/month in revenue.
> 
> Not sure if this applies to [Company], but if you're interested, I'd be happy to show you how it works. No pitch, just a quick screenshare.
> 
> Worth 15 minutes?
> 
> — Alex

---

### Message 2: Case Study (If no reply after 3 days)

> Hey [First Name],
> 
> Not sure if you saw my last message, but wanted to share something I thought might be relevant.
> 
> I just wrapped up a project with [Similar Business Type] in Ridgewood. They were losing leads because:
> - Front desk couldn't answer every call
> - Website inquiries sat for hours
> - After-hours calls went straight to voicemail
> 
> We built them an AI system that:
> - Answers inquiries 24/7
> - Books appointments automatically
> - Sends reminders to reduce no-shows
> 
> **Result:** 40% increase in booked appointments within 90 days.
> 
> If [Company] is facing anything similar, I'd be happy to show you how we did it. Takes about 15 minutes.
> 
> Let me know if you're open to it.
> 
> — Alex

### Message 3: Breakup (If no reply after 7 days)

> Hey [First Name],
>
