About Student Loan Forgiveness Programs

Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF)

  • Forgives remaining balance after 120 qualifying payments (10 years)
  • Must work full-time for qualifying employer (government or 501(c)(3) nonprofit)
  • Payments must be made under an income-driven repayment plan
  • Forgiven amount is NOT taxable

Income-Driven Repayment (IDR) Forgiveness

  • Forgives remaining balance after 20-25 years of payments (depending on plan)
  • Available to all federal student loan borrowers
  • Payment based on income and family size (10-20% of discretionary income)
  • Forgiven amount MAY be taxable (check current tax laws)

Who Qualifies?

  • PSLF: Teachers, nurses, government employees, nonprofit workers
  • IDR: Any federal student loan borrower (Direct Loans)
  • Both require consistent, on-time payments
  • Must recertify income annually for IDR plans
