Understanding Business Structure

When calculating business taxes, it's important to understand:

  • Self-employment tax (15.3%) covers Social Security and Medicare
  • Income tax is calculated on net profit (income minus expenses)
  • Quarterly estimated tax payments help avoid penalties
  • Proper record-keeping is essential for deductions

Common Business Deductions

  • Home office expenses (simplified or actual method)
  • Vehicle and mileage (standard rate or actual expenses)
  • Equipment and supplies
  • Professional services and software subscriptions
