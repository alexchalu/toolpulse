About California Income Tax

California has one of the highest state income tax rates in the United States, with a progressive tax system ranging from 1% to 12.3%. High earners over $1 million also pay an additional 1% mental health services tax.

Key Features:

  • Progressive tax brackets from 1% to 12.3%
  • Standard deduction: $5,363 (single), $10,726 (married/head of household)
  • Additional 1% tax on income over $1 million
  • No local income taxes in California
  • Credits available for renters, earned income, and dependent care

Important Notes:

  • This calculator shows California state income tax only (not federal)
  • Actual tax may vary based on credits, exemptions, and other factors
  • Standard deduction amounts are indexed for inflation annually
  • Consult a tax professional for personalized advice
