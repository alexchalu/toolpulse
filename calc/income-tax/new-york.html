About New York Income Tax

New York has a progressive state income tax system with rates ranging from 4% to 10.9%. NYC residents also pay city income tax (3.078% to 3.876%).

Key Features:

  • Progressive tax brackets from 4% to 10.9%
  • Standard deduction: $8,000 (single), $16,050 (married), $11,200 (head of household)
  • NYC residents pay additional city income tax
  • Yonkers residents pay additional 16.75% surcharge on state tax
  • Credits available for earned income, child/dependent care, and college tuition

NYC Income Tax (Additional)

New York City residents pay additional local income tax:

  • $0 - $12,000: 3.078%
  • $12,001 - $25,000: 3.762%
  • $25,001 - $50,000: 3.819%
  • $50,001+: 3.876%

Important Notes:

  • This calculator shows NY state tax only (not federal or NYC)
  • NYC residents add 3.078% - 3.876% city tax
  • Yonkers residents pay 16.75% surcharge on state tax
  • Actual tax may vary based on credits and exemptions
  • Consult a tax professional for personalized advice
