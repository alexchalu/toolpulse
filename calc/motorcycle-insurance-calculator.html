Motorcycle Insurance Cost Factors

  • Type of Bike: Sport bikes cost 2-4x more to insure than cruisers
  • Age & Experience: Riders under 25 pay significantly higher premiums
  • Coverage Level: Liability-only is cheapest; full coverage costs 3-5x more
  • Driving Record: Accidents/tickets can double your premium
  • Location: Urban areas and states with high theft rates cost more

Average Motorcycle Insurance Cost (2026)

  • 🏍️ Sport Bike: $800-$2,000/year
  • 🏍️ Cruiser/Standard: $400-$800/year
  • 🏍️ Touring Bike: $600-$1,200/year
  • 🛵 Scooter: $200-$400/year

How to Lower Your Premium

  • 💰 Take a motorcycle safety course (10-15% discount)
  • 💰 Bundle with auto/home insurance
  • 💰 Install anti-theft devices
  • 💰 Store bike in a garage
  • 💰 Ride less miles per year
  • 💰 Pay annually instead of monthly
