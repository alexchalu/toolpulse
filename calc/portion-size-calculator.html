Meal Prep Tips

  • Use a food scale for accurate portions
  • Prep containers with divided sections for balanced meals
  • Cook in bulk and portion immediately while food is still hot
  • Label containers with date and calorie count
  • Store proteins and carbs separately from vegetables for better freshness
