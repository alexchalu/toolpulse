What is a Structured Settlement?

A structured settlement is a financial arrangement where a claim is settled through periodic payments over time rather than a lump sum. These settlements are common in personal injury cases, medical malpractice, and other legal claims.

Why Calculate Present Value?

Calculating the present value helps you understand what your future payments are worth today. This is crucial when considering whether to sell your structured settlement payments for a lump sum.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your total settlement amount
  2. Input your annual payment amount
  3. Specify how many years remain
  4. Adjust the discount rate (typically 4-8% for structured settlements)
  5. Click "Calculate Present Value"

Important Considerations

  • Present value calculations are estimates and may vary from actual offers
  • Discount rates typically range from 4% to 8% for structured settlements
  • Selling your structured settlement may result in receiving less than the present value
  • Consult with a financial advisor before making decisions
  • Check your state's laws regarding structured settlement transfers

State Regulations for Selling Structured Settlements

Each state has different laws regarding structured settlement transfers. Some states require court approval, while others have specific waiting periods. Always check your local regulations before proceeding.
