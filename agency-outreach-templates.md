## General Outreach Tips

### Best Practices for Cold Email:
1. **Personalize the first line** — reference their business name, location, or something specific you noticed
2. **Lead with their pain point** — not your solution
3. **Use specific numbers** — case study results make it credible
4. **One clear CTA** — just ask for a brief call
5. **Keep it short** — 200-300 words max
6. **Follow up 2-3 times** — most deals happen after follow-ups

### Follow-up Sequence:
- **Day 0:** Initial email
- **Day 3:** "Just following up" (bump the thread with a question)
- **Day 7:** Share a relevant case study or insight
- **Day 14:** "Should I close your file?" (breakup email)

### Subject Line Testing:
- **Curiosity:** "Quick question about [Company Name]"
- **Social proof:** "How [Similar Company] achieved [result]"
- **Direct benefit:** "[Specific outcome] in [timeframe]"
- **Question:** "Losing [X]% of leads to [problem]?"

## Targeting Strategy

### Best Prospects:
- **Revenue:** $500K - $10M annually (can afford automation, high ROI potential)
- **Team size:** 5-50 employees (pain is acute, but manageable deployment)
- **Lead volume:** 50+ inquiries/month (enough volume to see clear ROI)
- **Tech adoption:** Already using CRM/booking software (ready for automation)

### Where to Find Them:
1. Google Maps — local businesses in target industries
2. LinkedIn — filter by company size and industry
3. Yelp/industry directories — active businesses with reviews
4. Trade associations — insurance/real estate/HVAC organizations
5. Local business journals — growing companies being featured

### Disqualifying Signals:
- Solo practitioners (too small for enterprise pricing)
- Companies with in-house dev teams (will build themselves)
- Businesses with <20 leads/month (ROI won't be clear enough)
- Companies actively shopping for free/cheap solutions

## Handling Common Objections

**"We already have a chatbot"**
→ "That's great! Most chatbots are good at answering FAQs. Ours goes further — qualifying leads, booking appointments, and integrating with your existing systems. Would it make sense to see if there are gaps we could fill?"

**"How is this different from [competitor]?"**
→ "Great question. We're not a SaaS platform with templates. We build custom automation infrastructure tailored to your specific business processes. Think of us as an AI development firm, not a chatbot vendor."

**"This sounds expensive"**
→ "Investment starts at $2,500 for setup + $500/month. Most clients see ROI in 30-60 days. [Similar Business] added $40K/month in revenue — so the real question is, what's the cost of NOT automating?"

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Absolutely. What specifically do you need to evaluate? Is it budget, timing, or technical feasibility? I'm happy to provide more details on any of those."

**"Can you send me more information?"**
→ "Of course! To send you the most relevant info, can you tell me: What's your biggest challenge with lead capture/follow-up right now? And roughly how many inquiries do you get per month?"
