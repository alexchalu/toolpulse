Length Conversion FAQ

How do I convert miles to meters?

Multiply the value in miles by 1609.34. For example, 10 miles = 1.609e+04 meters.

What is 1 miles in meters?

How do I convert meters back to miles?

Divide the value in meters by 1609.34, or multiply by 0.000621371.
