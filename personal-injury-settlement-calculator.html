⚠️ Important Legal Information

Disclaimer: This calculator provides general educational information only and does not constitute legal advice. Settlement amounts vary widely based on jurisdiction, insurance company policies, evidence strength, and legal representation. For accurate settlement evaluation, consult with a licensed personal injury attorney in your state. Results are estimates only and may not reflect actual case outcomes.

Statute of Limitations: Each state has different deadlines for filing personal injury lawsuits. Check your state's laws to ensure you file within the required timeframe.

Contingency Fee: Most personal injury attorneys work on a contingency basis (typically 33-40% of the settlement). This means no upfront fees, payment only if you win your case.
