What This Calculator Does

Our Personal Injury Settlement Calculator helps you estimate potential compensation for personal injury cases. This includes:

  • Medical Bills - Past and future treatment costs
  • Lost Wages - Income you couldn't earn due to injury
  • Pain and Suffering - Compensation for physical and emotional distress
  • Liability Factors - How fault allocation affects your settlement
  • Settlement vs Trial - Estimates for negotiated settlements vs court awards

Why You Need This Tool

  • Calculate potential compensation ranges for your case
  • Understand how different factors (medical bills, lost wages, pain and suffering) contribute to your settlement estimate
  • Compare settlement offers from insurance companies vs potential court awards
  • Get realistic estimates based on case law and settlement data
